Time-Based Result Replay

Watch Your Poll Results Evolve Over Time

What is Time-Based Result Replay?

An interactive feature that lets you travel through time to see how poll results changed as votes were cast. Perfect for understanding voting patterns, trends, and momentum!

Key Features

Interactive Timeline Slider

Drag to any point in time to see results at that exact moment

Auto-Play Functionality

Watch results evolve automatically with customizable speed

Dynamic Charts

Pie and bar charts update in real-time as you navigate

Multiple Speeds

Control playback: 0.5x, 1x, 2x, or 4x speed

How It Works

1
Navigate to Poll Results

Go to any poll's results page. You'll see a new "Time Replay" button in the navigation.

2
Click Time Replay

Click the button to open the interactive replay interface.

3
Explore the Timeline

Use the controls to navigate through time and watch results change:

Jump to Start
Play/Pause
Jump to End

Drag the slider to any time point

4
Analyze the Data

Watch how vote percentages change, identify trends, and discover insights!

Use Cases

Track Voting Momentum

See when votes came in rapidly and identify periods of high engagement.

Detect Opinion Shifts

Watch how the leading option changed over time and understand why.

Correlate with Events

Match vote spikes with campaigns, announcements, or other events.

Educational Analysis

Demonstrate polling concepts, sampling effects, and temporal trends.

Understand Voter Behavior

Analyze when different voter segments participated in the poll.

Quick Start

Ready to Use!

The feature is installed and ready. Just visit any poll's results page and click "Time Replay"!

Example URLs:
  • results.php?poll_id=1 → Click "Time Replay" button
  • replay_results.php?poll_id=1 → Direct access
  • live_dashboard.php?poll_id=1 → Click "Time Replay" button
