HOW IT WORKS

1

Create & Invite

Log into the admin panel and click "Create Duet Poll". Select your collaborator from the list of admins, add your poll question and options, then send the invitation.

2

Accept Invitation

Your collaborator receives a notification and can view the invitation in their "Duet Polls" page. They can accept or decline with one click.

3

Collaborate & Manage

Once accepted, both creators have full access to edit, manage, and view results. All changes are tracked for transparency.

4

Share & Engage

Share your duet poll with your audience. The poll displays both creators' names, showing your collaborative effort.
